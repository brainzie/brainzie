Overview

Our third software-development edition brings new and returning students into one cohort. Rather than splitting the room, we differentiate the work: shared core ideas, with extension challenges for those further along and close support for those just starting — so everyone is genuinely stretched. This page will be expanded with highlights and outcomes once the cohort wraps up.

What the cohort is exploring

  • A shared foundation — core concepts every student needs, regardless of starting point.
  • Differentiated challenges — extension work for returning students, scaffolding for newcomers.
  • Project-based learning — building real things, not just exercises.
  • Professional habits — version control, clean code and debugging woven throughout.
