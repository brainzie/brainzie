Brainzie Poolside

The swimming coach's stopwatch. In training, swimmers leave the wall on staggered 5–10 second intervals — the first in every lane together, then the next, and the next — and one wall clock can't time them all. Poolside gives every swimmer their own accurate time, from one phone in the coach's hand.

Request early access
⏱️

What it does

🏊

One tap per send-off

Tap Start as each group leaves the wall — the 1st swimmer in every lane together, then the 2nd, then the 3rd. The clock starts with the first group.

⏱️

Individual times

Tap a lane's Stop as its swimmer finishes. Their own elapsed time — measured from their start, to the hundredth — freezes in place.

📣

Call out real times

No mental arithmetic at the end of the rep. Every swimmer's time is on screen, ready to read out — then one tap clears the board for the next rep.
